Kevin Shi

I am a Ph.D. student in the Theory Group in the Computer Science department at Columbia University, where I am fortunate to be co-advised by Daniel Hsu and Allison Bishop.

I received my B.A. and M.A. in Mathematics from the University of Pennsylvania, where I was also Chair of the Penn Undergraduate Math Society.

I am interested in machine learning and cryptography, particularly in bandits, online learning, nonconvex optimization.
